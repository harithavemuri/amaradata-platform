Features

Everything your team needs to run a property business

AmaraData replaces spreadsheets, chat threads, and fragmented tools with a single platform built for modern real estate teams.

Property Sales Pipeline

Track every lead from first inquiry to booking. Assign sales reps, update status (new → contacted → qualified → booked), and get full pipeline visibility across all projects.

Rental Management

Handle the full rental lifecycle — applications, agreements, and rent collection. Manage tenant and owner profiles, track maintenance requests, and coordinate vendors in one place.

Dynamic Pricing Engine

Calculate sale prices automatically — floor-rise charges, corner and facing premiums, club membership, and applicable taxes. Generate instant quotes for any unit in any project.

Payment Schedules & Tracking

Create milestone-based installment plans per buyer. Track payment status in real time, monitor collection aging, and maintain a complete payment history from booking to registration.

Multi-Project Inventory

Manage multiple projects with independent building, tower, and unit structures. Track floor plans, unit types, dimensions, parking allocations, and live availability — all in one view.

Reports & Analytics

Sales performance dashboards, payment collection aging, and occupancy reports — filterable by project, date range, or sales rep. Export to PDF or spreadsheet with one click.

How It Works

Up and running in three steps

Get your entire real estate operation organised — projects, sales, and rentals — in a single afternoon.

1

Set Up Your Projects

Add your projects, buildings, and unit inventory. Configure pricing rules — floor rise, corner premiums, local taxes — once, and they apply automatically everywhere.

2

Manage Sales & Rentals

Track leads through the sales pipeline, generate price quotes, execute rental agreements, collect payments, and handle maintenance — all from one dashboard.

3

Invoice & Report

Generate tax-compliant invoices with one click. Get instant reports on sales performance, payment aging, and occupancy across every project.
